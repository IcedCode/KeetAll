Rules Last Updated May 4, 2015

A. General Composure

  1. 1. Maintain a pleasant, casual atmosphere. Be respectful and use common sense. Excessive swearing, rudeness and harassment is not tolerated.
  2. 2. Try to maintain proper spelling and grammar.
  3. 3. Do not spam.
  4. 4. Do not link to any of the following:
    1. a. Objectionable material, which includes but is not limited to pornography, "shock sites", viruses, phishing sites and hate groups.
    2. b. Other websites or servers in a manner that constitutes advertising.
    3. c. Personal identifying information of other users.
  5. 5. Do not encourage or partake in rule breaking on Keet Network services.
  6. 6. Do not impersonate any member of the Keet Network staff, or claim to be a staff member.
  7. 7. Report issues or bugs of Keet Network services to either the forums (http://thetowersne.zz.mu/forum) or to support@thetowers.zz.mu; exploitation of bugs for any reason other than to demonstrate at the request of a staff member will result in a punishment.
  8. 8. Follow any directions given by a Keet Network staff member. If you believe a staff member has directed you wrongly, please email support@thetowers.zz.mu
    9. *Note -If you are concerned about links that may constitute advertising, please clarify with a staff member before posting.
